I’m a data scientist based in Denver, CO. In August 2020 I received my PhD in Applied Math from CU Boulder, under my advisor Vanja Dukic. My thesis work applied random matrix methods to problems at the intersection of ecology and epidemiology [1]. I have also worked with the Sensing and Predictive Analytics Group at the National Renewable Energy Lab to apply statistical methods to problems in grid integration of renewable energy [2,3].
